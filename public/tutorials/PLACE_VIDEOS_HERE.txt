Place your tutorial videos in this folder with the following names:

- memory-game.mp4
- whack-a-mole.mp4
- colour-sequence.mp4
- math-game.mp4
- word-search.mp4
- number-sorting.mp4
- castle-defense.mp4
- tic-tac-toe.mp4
- connect4.mp4
- rhythm-game.mp4

Video Requirements:
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec recommended for browser compatibility)
- Duration: 30-60 seconds maximum
- Resolution: 1280x720 or 1920x1080
- File size: Keep under 10MB per video for fast loading
- Audio: Optional (can add voiceover or keep silent)

Tips for recording:
1. Screen record one complete game round
2. Show clear examples of gameplay
3. Use large, readable text overlays
4. Highlight where to click/tap
5. Keep it simple and short
